Palani’s sandy beach is wide and long enough to host a national beach football competition. Palani Beach is said to be a “paradise free from the blight of congestion in popular white-beach destinations.” Palani Beach is “swimmer-friendly” since its sandy beach falls gradually to the sea, with the water only waist-deep even at 500 meters from the shoreline.
